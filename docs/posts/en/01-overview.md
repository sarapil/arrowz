# 📞 Arrowz — Unified Communications for ERPNext

> **One app. Voice calls. Video meetings. WhatsApp. Telegram. Network management. All inside ERPNext.**

---

## The Problem

Modern businesses juggle 5-7 different tools for communication:
- 📞 A PBX system for phone calls
- 💬 WhatsApp Business for customer messaging
- 📱 Telegram for team coordination
- 🎥 Zoom/Teams for video meetings
- 🌐 A separate dashboard for network/WiFi management
- 📊 And somehow, none of it connects to your ERP

**The result?** Missed calls with no CRM records. Customer conversations lost across platforms. Network issues that take hours to diagnose because the tools are disconnected.

## The Solution: Arrowz

Arrowz brings **everything** into one Frappe/ERPNext workspace:

### 🎙️ WebRTC Softphone
- Browser-based calling — no external software needed
- Click-to-call from any ERPNext contact, lead, or customer
- Automatic call logging with duration, recordings, and notes
- FreePBX/Asterisk integration via AMI

### 💬 Omni-Channel Messaging
- **WhatsApp Cloud API** — send/receive directly from ERPNext
- **Telegram Bot** — automated notifications and two-way chat
- Unified inbox — all conversations in one timeline
- Auto-link messages to customers, leads, and support tickets

### 🎥 Video Conferencing
- OpenMeetings integration — no Zoom/Teams license needed
- Create meeting rooms directly from ERPNext
- Share meeting links with customers via WhatsApp/Email
- Record meetings and attach to relevant documents

### 🌐 Network Management
- **MikroTik RouterOS** — full router management from ERPNext
- WiFi management, bandwidth control, firewall rules
- Real-time monitoring dashboards
- Multi-site network topology visualization

### 📊 Everything Connected
- Every call, message, and meeting automatically linked to ERPNext records
- Customer communication timeline across ALL channels
- AI-powered call summaries and sentiment analysis (Professional+)
- Team performance analytics and SLA tracking
